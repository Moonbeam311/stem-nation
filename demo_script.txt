Welcome to STEM Nation.

This is a live simulation platform where students learn governance, economics, and strategy by building civilizations in real time.

We begin in the Command Center.

Here, students observe the state of their civilization, including population, resources, and stability.

Now, we advance the simulation.

Each turn introduces new conditions that require strategic thinking.

Next, we make a decision.

Every choice impacts the system, creating real consequences.

Now we move to the Strategic Map.

Territories contain resources such as food, trade, and defense.

Control of territory shapes the future of the civilization.

Finally, we return to the Command Center to observe the results.

This is not memorization.

This is decision-based learning.

This is STEM Nation.
