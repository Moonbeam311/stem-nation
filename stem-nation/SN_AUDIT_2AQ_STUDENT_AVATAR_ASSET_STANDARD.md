# SN-AUDIT-2AQ — Student Avatar Asset Standard

## Decision

The avatar system must not rely on emoji or generic placeholder portraits as the final experience.

The current SVG files in `/static/avatars/` are temporary placeholders only.

## Final Avatar Direction

The student avatar layer should use Council-style illustrated portraits that match the STEM Nation world.

## Required Avatar Roster

1. Young Scout
2. Young Builder
3. Young Caretaker
4. Young Mapkeeper
5. Young Inventor
6. Young Diplomat

## Visual Standard

Each avatar should be:

- cartoon / animated-series quality
- student-facing and middle-grade friendly
- consistent with the STEM Nation Council visual universe
- expressive, warm, and heroic
- rendered as a portrait/card image
- not emoji-based
- not generic clipart
- visually distinct by role, posture, clothing, tool, and symbol

## Palette

Use the STEM Nation palette:

- deep navy
- warm gold
- earth browns
- river blue
- forest green
- muted academy parchment tones

## File Names

Final files should replace or upgrade:

- `/static/avatars/student_scout.png`
- `/static/avatars/student_builder.png`
- `/static/avatars/student_caretaker.png`
- `/static/avatars/student_mapkeeper.png`
- `/static/avatars/student_inventor.png`
- `/static/avatars/student_diplomat.png`

Temporary SVG placeholders may remain as fallback files.
