Survival Scenario 1: Leader Command Trial
Time until nightfall: 40 minutes

You are the leader before nightfall.

Your small group has reached unfamiliar land. The food is limited. The water jars are nearly empty. The group is tired, and night is coming.

You carry only a few survival materials: rolled shelter cloth, food sacks, water jars, rope, wooden stakes, small tools, fire starter or matches, a blanket, and marker stones or a torn map scrap.

You cannot spread everything everywhere. You must decide which materials go with each specialist, send them to inspect the land, read their reports, and then build a first-night survival plan.

ScoutStudies movement, danger signs, paths, distance, and animal tracks.
BuilderStudies shelter, dry ground, flat land, wood, stone, and flood protection.
GathererStudies food, water, fish, plants, storage safety, and animal signs.
CaretakerStudies health, rest, clean water, warmth, injury risk, and safe overnight conditions.
