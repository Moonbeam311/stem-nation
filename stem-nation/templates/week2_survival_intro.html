STEM Nation · Week 2

The Individual & Survival

Your people have a place to begin. Now they must survive there. A settlement is not a civilization until food, shelter, labor, and risk are managed.

Survival Pressure Begins

Food:
How will your people eat before reserves run out?
Shelter:
How will your people protect themselves from weather, danger, and exposure?
Labor:
Who gathers, builds, watches, records, and plans?
Scarcity:
What happens when there is not enough for everyone?
Risk:
What threat must be handled first?
Consequence:
Every early choice changes what your civilization can become.
Week 2 begins with one truth: survival is the first test of civilization.
