Student Task:
Identify your chosen region, explain its strongest advantage, name its greatest risk, and describe how your people will survive the first stage of civilization.

This screen is the Week 1 bridge between map observation and the future live simulation engine.

1. Observe the Land

What did the map reveal? Look for water, food sources, movement paths, barriers, and dangerous terrain.

2. Identify Resources

Decide which resources matter first: food, shelter, defense, trade access, or room to grow.

3. Name the Risk

Every region has weakness. Explain the challenge your people must overcome to survive.

4. Defend the Decision

Use evidence from the map to justify your settlement choice. Strong leaders explain their reasoning.
