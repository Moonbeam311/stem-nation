Mission Briefing

Mission Coach
Study the incident. Your group cannot safely go back before dark.

Your group was traveling along an old route when rising water and mud loosened the riverbank. The crossing behind you became unsafe. Part of the path washed out, and the group cannot safely return before dark.

The group has 12 people: four adults, three older youth, two young children, two elders, and one injured traveler.

The youngest are tired. The elders are moving slowly. The injured traveler needs rest. Some supplies are damp. The water jars are nearly empty.

Tradeoff Doctrine
No survival plan removes all danger. Every decision protects something while risking something else.
