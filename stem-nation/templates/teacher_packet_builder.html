Teacher Packet Builder

The Teacher Packet Builder is the future classroom assembly system for combining resources, facilitation guidance, lesson framing, and student archive artifacts into structured instructional packets.

Packet Builder Purpose

Teachers should eventually be able to build customized classroom packets that combine lesson plans, primary sources, maps, facilitation guidance, discussion prompts, archive artifacts, and district-alignment notes into a single instructional workflow.
